## 📊 MODULE 1: DASHBOARD

Show a real-time overview with:

### KPI Cards (top row):
- Total Revenue (this month)
- Total Outstanding (unpaid invoices)
- Total Expenses (this month)
- Number of Active Customers

### Charts:
- Bar chart: Monthly Revenue vs Expenses (last 6 months)
- Donut chart: Invoice Status Breakdown (Paid / Unpaid / Overdue / Draft)
- Line chart: Payments received over time (last 30 days)

### Tables:
- Recent Invoices (last 5) with status badge
- Top 5 Customers by revenue
- Overdue Invoices alert list

---

## 👥 MODULE 2: CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT

### Customer List:
- Table with columns: Name, Company, Phone, Email, Country, Total Billed, Status
- Search, filter by status (Active/Inactive), sort by name or total billed
- Pagination

### Add/Edit Customer Form:
- Contact Name, Company Name
- Email, Phone, WhatsApp Number
- Billing Address, Country
- Currency (default AED)
- Payment Terms (Net 15 / Net 30 / Net 45 / Due on Receipt)
- Notes / Internal remarks
- Status toggle (Active/Inactive)

### Customer Detail Page:
- Customer info summary
- Transaction history tab (all invoices, quotes, payments)
- Outstanding balance display

---

## 📦 MODULE 3: PRODUCT / ITEM CATALOG

### Product List:
- Table: Item Name, SKU, Category, Unit Price (AED), Tax Rate, Stock Unit, Status
- Search and filter by category
- Categories relevant to TIARA: Auto Spare Parts, Lubricants & Engine Oils, 
  Car Care Products, Vehicle Maintenance, General Trading, Industrial Supplies

### Add/Edit Product Form:
- Item Name, SKU/Item Code
- Category (dropdown from above list)
- Description
- Unit (Pcs / Ltr / Kg / Box / Set)
- Unit Price (AED)
- Tax Rate (0% or 5% VAT)
- Status (Active/Inactive)

---

## 📋 MODULE 4: QUOTES / ESTIMATES

### Quote List:
- Table: Quote #, Customer, Date, Expiry Date, Amount, Status
- Status badges: Draft / Sent / Accepted / Declined / Expired
- Filter by status, search by customer or quote number

### Create/Edit Quote:
- Auto-generate Quote Number (QT-0001, QT-0002...)
- Select Customer (dropdown from Customers)
- Quote Date, Expiry Date
- Line Items table:
  - Select Product (auto-fill price & tax)
  - Description, Qty, Unit Price, Tax %, Discount %, Line Total
  - Add/remove rows dynamically
- Subtotal, Total Discount, Total VAT (5%), Grand Total
- Notes / Terms & Conditions field
- Status management: Save as Draft → Mark as Sent → Accept / Decline

### Quote Actions:
- Convert accepted Quote → Invoice (one click, auto-populate all fields)
- Download as PDF
- Send via Email (mailto link)

---

## 🧾 MODULE 5: INVOICES

### Invoice List:
- Table: Invoice #, Customer, Date, Due Date, Amount, Status
- Status badges: Draft / Sent / Paid / Partially Paid / Overdue
- Filter by status, date range, customer
- Bulk status update

### Create/Edit Invoice:
- Auto-generate Invoice Number (INV-0001, INV-0002...)
- Select Customer
- Invoice Date, Due Date (auto-calculated from payment terms)
- Reference / PO Number field
- Line Items (same as Quote — product selector with auto-fill)
- Subtotal, Discount, VAT (5%), Grand Total
- Notes / Payment Instructions
- Status: Draft → Sent → Paid

### Invoice Actions:
- Download/Print as PDF (well-formatted with the attached company logo,
  TIARA details, customer details, line items, VAT breakdown)
- Mark as Paid (full or partial)
- Send via Email (mailto)
- View payment history for the invoice

---

## 💰 MODULE 6: PAYMENT TRACKING

### Payments List:
- Table: Payment #, Invoice #, Customer, Date, Amount, Method, Status
- Payment Methods: Bank Transfer / Cash / Cheque / Online
- Search by customer or invoice

### Record Payment:
- Link to Invoice
- Payment Date
- Amount Received
- Payment Method
- Reference Number (bank ref / cheque number)
- Notes
- Support partial payments (invoice stays "Partially Paid" until fully settled)

### Outstanding Summary:
- Total outstanding by customer
- Overdue invoices (past due date and unpaid)

- Expense Date
- Category (dropdown)
- Vendor / Supplier Name
- Description
- Amount (AED)
- VAT (5% toggle — is this VAT inclusive?)
- Payment Method (Cash / Bank / Credit Card)

- Notes
- Status: Pending / Approved / Rejected

### Expense Summary:
- Monthly expense total
- Breakdown by category (donut chart on expense list page)

## 📈 MODULE 8: REPORTS

### Available Reports:
1. **Sales Summary** – Total invoiced, collected, outstanding by month
2. **Invoice Aging Report** – Overdue invoices grouped by 0-30, 31-60, 
   61-90, 90+ days
3. **Expense Report** – Expenses by category and month
4. **Customer Ledger** – All transactions (invoices + payments) for a 
   selected customer
5. **VAT Summary** – Total VAT collected on invoices vs VAT on expenses 
   (simple format for UAE VAT filing)

### Report Features:
- Date range filter (This Month / Last Month / This Quarter / Custom)
- Export as CSV / Print view
- Summary KPI at top of each report

## 🎨 UI/UX DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary / Accent:** Yellow #FFB000 — used for buttons, active sidebar 
  items, highlights, badges, chart accents
- **Background / Base:** Black #000000 — used for sidebar background, 
  header, login page background
- **Surface / Content Area:** Light Gray #EEEEEE — used for main content 
  background, cards, table rows
- **Text on dark:** White #FFFFFF
- **Text on light:** Black #000000
- **Borders:** #DDDDDD (subtle)

### Logo:
- Use the attached logo image throughout the app:
  - Top-left of the sidebar (white/transparent version if needed)
  - Login page center
  - PDF invoice header

### Layout:
- Sidebar navigation (collapsible), fixed left
- Top header bar: page title left, user name + role + logout button right
- Main content area in Light Gray #EEEEEE
- Cards and tables with white backgrounds and subtle shadows

### Sidebar Design:
- Background: Black #000000
- Logo at top
- Nav items: white text, yellow #FFB000 icon/left-border on active item
- Hover state: slight yellow tint background

### Login Page:
- Full black background
- Centered card in white/light gray
- Logo prominently at top of card
- Yellow #FFB000 primary button
- Demo credentials shown below form

### Buttons:
- Primary: Yellow #FFB000 background, black text, rounded
- Secondary: Black background, white text, rounded
- Danger: Red #EF4444 for delete actions

### Responsive: Works on desktop and tablet

### Sidebar menu items by role:
- Admin: Dashboard, Customers, Products, Quotes, Invoices, Payments, 
  Expenses, Reports, Settings
- Sales Manager: Dashboard, Customers, Products, Quotes, Invoices, Payments

### Status badges with color coding:
- Draft → Gray
- Sent → Blue
- Paid → Green
- Overdue → Red
- Pending → Yellow #FFB000
- Accepted → Green
- Declined → Red

### UX Details:
- Empty states with helpful messages when no data exists
- Confirmation dialogs before delete actions
- Toast notifications for success/error actions
- All monetary values displayed in AED format (e.g. AED 1,250.00)
